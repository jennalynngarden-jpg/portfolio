Robinhood

First trade recommendations

Around a third of customers who join Robinhood never place a trade. User research tells us that many of them hesitate because they lack the domain knowledge and confidence to decide what to invest in.

We created an experience where customers answer questions, learn their investment risk level based on responses, and receive recommendations for a diversified portfolio of four exchange-traded funds matching their risk profile. They can then invest through bulk checkout. Compliance with SEC's Regulation Best Interest was required.

Role Senior Content Designer
Team Product, Design, Legal, Compliance

My role

I led end-to-end UX content design, provided feedback on product requirements and early designs, iterated during user research, wrote all customer communications and Help Center content, and designed the in-app customer support triage flow while collaborating with five Legal and Compliance partners.

This was the first product to transition Robinhood from an entirely self-directed broker-dealer to one that makes recommendations to customers. That's a big deal! With so many eyes on the project from all sides of the business, I had to be able to clearly articulate my rationale for every content decisionâ€”even seemingly small ones!

Success factors included gradual information progression, narrative storytelling, and functional visuals aligned with content.

Legal and compliance process

Required sign-off from 2 lawyers and 3 Compliance principals across 200+ screens, customer communications, and a Help Center article.

I grouped content pieces for approval, established a review cadence, and balanced different communication styles, availabilities, and competing priorities. I met multiple times weekly to understand and address feedback, articulating rationale for nearly every content decision. I learned to constructively challenge disagreeable feedback.

The outcome: strengthened relationships with stakeholders and improved content design abilities.

Help Center article

A product operations manager helped anticipate and prioritize customer questions. I organized questions into sections with drafted responses and collaborated with Legal and Compliance partners to incorporate feedback.

The article was intentionally comprehensive to balance customer education with transparency regarding investment recommendations.

Outcomes

We drove a statistically significant increase in first trade and long-term activity rates among our target audience.

+3.2%
increase in first trade rate among investors with less experience
+7.7%
increase in long-term activity (continued buying/holding) among investors with less experience

Flow completion was higher among less-experienced investors. We also saw higher adoption among women and investors under age 20.
