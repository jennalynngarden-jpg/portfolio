Growing Robinhood Gold

Robinhood Gold is a $5/month membership offering interest on uninvested cash, premium research, and investment tools. Despite these features, the company struggled with internal marketing and conversion. I partnered with Product Management, Design, and Data Science to design and test growth experiments that increased Gold subscribers and deposits.

Role Senior Content Designer
Team Product, Design, Data Science

Strategic framework

Three core principles guided our approach:

  1. Value Proposition Focus: Emphasize the competitive APY benefit over less universally appealing features
  2. Contextual Integration: Place upsells within existing user flows naturally
  3. Tangible Value: Leverage earnings projections to demonstrate concrete benefits

I established a formalized growth design workflow covering hypothesis definition, experiment design, stakeholder management, and documentation. The playbook became an onboarding resource, typically enabling teams to move from conception to implementation in 2-3 weeks.

Experiment 1: Native funding upsell

Previous onboarding Gold upsells achieved 40% adoption lift using a choice selector design positioned after funding.

Hypothesis: "If we tie the Gold upsell more closely to funding, more users will sign up for Gold."

Design iterations

  • Variant 1: Repositioned the selector design within the funding flow (post-deposit), maintaining visual consistency
  • Variant 2: Moved the upsell pre-deposit with explanatory messaging about upcoming deposit benefits, using familiar design patterns

Experiment 2: Earnings calculator for Gold members

Goal: Reinforce positive purchase decisions and encourage larger deposits.

Hypothesis: Contextually highlighting potential Gold earnings during deposits would increase deposit amounts.

Design approach

A banner below the deposit input combined interest earnings and deposit-bonus projections, with additional legal disclosure available behind an interactive element. The design balanced compliance requirements with readable messaging.

Legal negotiation

I convinced compliance to display combined earnings figures rather than separated ones, using constraint communication to justify the approach.

5-10% net deposit increase
for users with >$2K linked accounts (3-7% for >$1K accounts). Results replicated after the deposit-boost feature deprecation.

Experiment 3: Earnings calculator for non-Gold members

The existing transfers flow couldn't accommodate the banner design due to space and sensitivity constraints.

Strategy

I implemented a contextual bottom sheet appearing post-transfer, targeted only to users depositing above the "breakeven point" (~$1,100), where interest earnings would offset the monthly fee.

Content design

User research informed messaging testing, ultimately favoring straightforward language ("you could earn") over cognitive-load-heavy alternatives about the fee offsetting itself. Legal concerns about projections led to hedged language and value-focused CTAs.

3.49% increase
in Gold adoption (statistically significant), with a directional 0.91% increase in net deposits.

Design principles applied

Across experiments, the work emphasized:

  • Compliance integration as a design constraint requiring creative solutions
  • Audience segmentation for relevance
  • Value-forward messaging and CTAs
  • Iterative testing with data-driven refinement
  • Context-native placements over disruptive overlays
